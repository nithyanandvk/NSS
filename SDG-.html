Government Schemes for Rural Development

Schemes for Children (0-6 years)

Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS)

Objective: Improve child health, nutrition, and education

Key Features: Provides food, preschool education, and healthcare through Anganwadi centers.

Eligibility: Children under 6 years

More Info: ICDS Official Website

National Nutrition Mission (POSHAN Abhiyaan)

Objective: Improve nutritional outcomes and reduce malnutrition

Key Features: Focuses on reducing stunting, undernutrition, and anemia among children.

Eligibility: Children under 6 years

More Info: POSHAN Abhiyaan Official Website

Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK)

Objective: Early identification and treatment of diseases in children

Key Features: Free health check-ups for conditions like anemia, vision, hearing issues, and other developmental problems.

Eligibility: Children under 18 years

More Info: RBSK Official Website

Schemes for School Students (6-18 years)

Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDMS)

Objective: Improve nutritional status and increase school attendance

Key Features: Provides free meals to students in government schools to encourage attendance and improve child nutrition.

Eligibility: School-going children (6-14 years)

More Info: MDM Official Website

Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA)

Objective: Universalize elementary education

Key Features: Provides free education, improves school infrastructure, and provides resources for teacher training.

Eligibility: School children (6-14 years)

More Info: SSA Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Objective: Skill development for employment

Key Features: Offers short-term training courses in various skills to improve employability.

Eligibility: Youth and dropouts (18+)

More Info: PMKVY Official Website

Schemes for Women (18+ years)

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Objective: Financial assistance for the first living child

Key Features: Cash incentive of ₹5,000 for healthcare and nutrition during pregnancy.

Eligibility: Pregnant and lactating women for their first child.

More Info: PMMVY Official Website

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP)

Objective: Promote girl child protection and education

Key Features: Campaign for saving and educating the girl child, reducing child sex ratio, and providing education support.

Eligibility: Girl children

More Info: BBBP Official Website

Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – NRLM

Objective: Empowerment of women through self-help groups

Key Features: Encourages the formation of Self Help Groups (SHGs) for entrepreneurship and financial independence.

Eligibility: Rural women

More Info: NRLM Official Website

Mahila E-Haat

Objective: Provide a platform for rural women entrepreneurs

Key Features: An online platform for rural women to sell products directly to consumers.

Eligibility: Rural women entrepreneurs

More Info: Mahila E-Haat Official Website

Schemes for Farmers (18+ years)

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Objective: Financial assistance to farmers

Key Features: Provides ₹6,000 annually in three equal installments directly to farmers' bank accounts.

Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers

More Info: PM-KISAN Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Objective: Crop insurance for natural calamities

Key Features: Provides insurance coverage for crops against natural disasters, pests, and diseases.

Eligibility: Farmers

More Info: PMFBY Official Website

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

Objective: Easy credit for agriculture needs

Key Features: Credit card allowing farmers to access loans for crop production, seeds, fertilizers, etc.

Eligibility: Farmers

More Info: KCC Official Website

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Objective: Promote organic farming

Key Features: Provides financial assistance and training for adopting organic farming practices.

Eligibility: Organic farmers

More Info: PKVY Official Website

Schemes for General Rural Population

Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

Objective: Provide guaranteed wage employment

Key Features: 100 days of wage employment annually to rural households for building infrastructure like roads, irrigation, etc.

Eligibility: Rural households

More Info: MGNREGA Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G)

Objective: Affordable housing for all

Key Features: Financial assistance to build pucca houses for the rural poor.

Eligibility: Homeless and poor families

More Info: PMAY-G Official Website

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY)

Objective: Improve rural electricity access

Key Features: Ensures reliable and quality electricity supply to rural households and farms.

Eligibility: Rural population

More Info: DDUGJY Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)

Objective: Improve rural road connectivity

Key Features: Connects rural areas with all-weather roads to promote social and economic development.

Eligibility: Rural population

More Info: PMGSY Official Website
